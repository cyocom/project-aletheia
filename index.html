FRC 2026 — OPR by team

Each row shows a team’s highest OPR at any official 2026 event (regionals, districts, championships, etc.—not preseason or offseason) and their OPR at the calendar-latest such event (from The Blue Alliance). Trimmed (latest) uses one global qual least squares fit at that event, then for each team drops up to three of their worst-residual matches and **refits only that team’s number** while everyone else stays at the global coefficients (see trimmedOprNote in the JSON). Regenerate data/teams-opr-2026.json with npm run fetch-data after setting TBA_AUTH_KEY.
